Enforced disappearance is defined under international law as the arrest or abduction of a person by the state or its agents, followed by a refusal to acknowledge the detention or to reveal the person's fate.

This page records publicly reported cases, with dates and districts, and links to the original documentation. Where a detail cannot be verified, it is marked rather than asserted.
