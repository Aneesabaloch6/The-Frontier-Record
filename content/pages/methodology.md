## Standards

Every entry on this site is tied to a source. The aim is documentation, not
assertion: a reader should be able to follow each claim back to where it came
from.

- Primary documents (court filings, official notices, first-hand testimony) are
  preferred over secondary reporting.
- Where only a single source exists, the entry is marked *unconfirmed*.
- Corrections are published openly rather than edited away silently.
