## What this record covers

Enforced disappearance is defined under international law as the arrest,
detention, or abduction of a person by the state or its agents, followed by a
refusal to acknowledge the deprivation of liberty or to disclose the person's
fate or whereabouts.

This page collects publicly reported cases, the dates and districts involved,
and links to the original documentation. Where a detail cannot be verified, it
is flagged rather than asserted.
